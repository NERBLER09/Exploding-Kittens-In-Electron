How To Play

Welcome To Exploding Kittens In Electron

If this is your first time playing Exploding Kittens or first time using Exploding Kittens In Electron this will guide you getting to know how to play and the quirks

The Game Layout

1. Your cards - This is where all you cards are shown

2. The draw and discard pile - This is where the draw and discard piles are

3. Remaining card - Show the amount of remaining cards in the deck

4. Computer players - Tells you how much computer players are in the game

Playing The Game

Now, for the moment you've been waiting for, lets learn how to play Exploding Kitten!

1. Click on a card in your hand, a pop up will show telling you what had happened.

Unlike some card games in Exploding Kittens you can play as many cards as you want. Play/pass, then draw.

2. Once you fell like you have did everything you with your turn, you can draw a card by clicking on the draw pile located at the center of the screen.

3. A popup will show telling you want card you've draws, a few other popups will tell you want card the computer players played and if it's your turn.

Repeat steps 1-3 until a player explodes

Cards:

Nope

Cancels what ever card a computer player has played and forces them to draw a card

Attack

Skips your turn and gives the next computer player 2 additional turns

Skip

Skips your turn and makes it be the next computer player's turn

Shuffle

Shuffles the deck

See the future

Shows you the top 3 cards

"Cat" cards

When having 2 matching cat cards it can be used to steal from a computer player of choice

Draw from the bottom

Draws the card from the bottom of the deck

Alter the future

Shows you the top 3 cards and allows you to reorder them

Targeted Attack

Skips your turn and and gives 2 additional turns to a computer player of your choice. Play starts from the chosen target.

Super super

Skips you turn and all other turns you may have

Catomic bomb

Skips your turn and brings all the Exploding Kittens in the deck to the top

See the future x5

Shows the top 5 cards

Alter the future x5

Shows you the top 5 cards and allows you to rearrange them

Swap top and bottom

Swaps the top and card of the deck

Streaking Kitten

Allows you have hold 1 Exploding Kitten card in your hand without exploding
