How To Play

Welcome To Exploding Kittens In Electron

If this is your first time playing Exploding Kittens or first time using Exploding Kittens In Electron this will guide you getting to know how to play and the quirks

The Game Layout

1. Your cards - This is where all you cards are shown

2. The draw and discard pile - This is where the draw and discard piles are

3. Remaining card - Show the amount of remaining cards in the deck

4. Computer players - Tells you how much computer players are in the game

Playing The Game

Now, for the moment you've been waiting for, lets learn how to play Exploding Kitten!

1. Click on a card in your hand, a pop up will show telling you what had happened.

Unlike some card games in Exploding Kittens you can play as many cards as you want. Play/pass, then draw.

2. Once you fell like you have did everything you with your turn, you can draw a card by clicking on the draw pile located at the center of the screen.

3. A popup will show telling you want card you've draws, a few other popups will tell you want card the computer players played and if it's your turn.

Repeat steps 1-3 until a player explodes
